# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project is committed to a professional, respectful, and constructive collaboration environment.

Participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully;
- critique ideas and artifacts, not people;
- assume good faith while still reviewing rigorously;
- welcome evidence-based disagreement;
- avoid harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory conduct;
- preserve the engineering integrity of the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- personal attacks;
- harassment or intimidation;
- discriminatory language or conduct;
- deliberate disruption;
- misuse of project spaces;
- knowingly submitting misleading or harmful content.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, reject, or moderate contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may result in restricted participation.
