## Purpose

This artifact records material AI assistance in the LMU/COICP engineering work.

It is not an audit of every keystroke. It records AI use that materially affects engineering artifacts, implementation choices, tests, analysis, release evidence, or governance decisions.

## AI posture

| Area | Current Position |
|---|---|
| Product AI feature | AI Incident Summary disabled through RC-001. |
| Engineering AI assistance | Allowed when reviewed, corrected, and accepted by humans. |
| Human accountability | Required for decisions, release readiness, governance, and official evidence. |
| Future AI enablement | Requires a separate readiness cycle. |

## AI usage register

| ID | Stage | Use | Human Review | Evidence Impact | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| AI-001 | Requirements | Drafted and refined scenario-specific requirement language. | Product Owner / Product Engineer | Requirements baseline improved for lineage. | Accepted |
| AI-002 | Architecture | Explored evidence-flow alternatives and AI-disabled boundary implications. | Architecture Review Board chair | Architecture decisions clarified. | Accepted |
| AI-003 | Design | Assisted with interface, workflow, data, and exception design drafting. | Product Engineer / reviewers | Design artifacts made scenario-specific. | Accepted |
| AI-004 | Implementation | Assisted with implementation planning and evidence structure. | Product Engineer | Implementation evidence reviewed. | Accepted |
| AI-005 | Testing | Assisted test-case coverage and traceability review. | Test lead / Product Engineer | Testing matrix and defect route strengthened. | Accepted |
| AI-006 | Governance | Assisted synthesis of governance gaps and next-cycle routing. | Product Owner / governance reviewers | Governance summary clarified. | Accepted |

## Required control

AI-assisted content is not official because AI produced it. It becomes project evidence only when responsible humans review, correct, accept, and preserve it in the repository.
