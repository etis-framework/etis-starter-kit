# Page Standard

Every platform page should answer these questions quickly:

1. Where am I?
2. Why does this page exist?
3. What should I do or understand?
4. What evidence or decision matters?
5. Where do I go next?

## Required page elements

Most engineering pages include:

- Stage Banner;
- Metadata Strip;
- Engineering Navigation;
- H1 page title;
- body content;
- relevant callouts;
- Continue Panel.

## Page categories

- Narrative pages teach.
- Reference pages define.
- Operational pages guide action.
- Manifest pages define stage contracts.
- Readiness pages evaluate advancement.
