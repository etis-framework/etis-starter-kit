# Writing Standard

ETIS platform writing should be calm, direct, and engineering-first.

## Voice

Use a mentoring engineering voice.

Avoid marketing language, hype, academic over-explanation, and decorative prose.

## Sentence discipline

Prefer short paragraphs.

Use lists when they improve scanability.

Do not repeat a concept unless repetition supports learning.

## Standard stance

AI may assist, but engineers remain accountable.
