# Shared Component — AI Assistance Box

Use this component when AI may help with a stage activity.

```markdown
🤖 **AI Assistance**

AI may help by:

- summarizing context;
- drafting candidate language;
- comparing alternatives;
- identifying risks;
- suggesting tests;
- reviewing for clarity.

AI must not replace:

- engineering judgment;
- stakeholder decisions;
- verification;
- approval;
- accountability.
```

## Required Principle

Every AI assistance section should reinforce:

> AI proposes; engineers verify.

## Rules

- Be specific about acceptable AI use.
- Identify where verification is required.
- Warn against using AI to skip engineering decisions.
- Do not imply that AI output is evidence unless it is reviewed and recorded.
