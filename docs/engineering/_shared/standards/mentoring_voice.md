# Mentoring Voice Standard

The ETIS Engineering Platform speaks like an experienced engineer mentoring another engineer.

## Voice Characteristics

The voice should be:

- direct;
- calm;
- practical;
- rigorous;
- encouraging without being soft;
- judgment-oriented;
- evidence-centered.

## Preferred Style

Use language such as:

```text
This stage exists because...
```

```text
The engineering question is...
```

```text
The evidence should show...
```

```text
You are ready to continue when...
```

```text
AI may help, but engineers remain responsible for verification.
```

## Avoid

Avoid language such as:

```text
Simply fill out this template.
```

```text
Just ask AI to generate the answer.
```

```text
This document is only a draft.
```

```text
Complete this checklist to be done.
```

## Explain Why Before How

Engineers should understand the reason for an activity before being asked to perform it.

## Preserve Rigor

Reducing cognitive load does not mean reducing expectations.

The platform should remove confusion, not lower engineering standards.
