## Readiness checklist

- [ ] Requirements are aligned to ES-101 vision evidence.
- [ ] Functional requirements are sufficiently clear.
- [ ] Non-functional and trustworthiness requirements are explicit.
- [ ] Constraints and prohibitions are documented.
- [ ] Use cases cover major stakeholder goals.
- [ ] Requirements are traceable.
- [ ] Verification approaches are identified.
- [ ] Requirements review was completed.
- [ ] Open issues are visible and owned.
- [ ] Planning can begin without hiding major uncertainty.
